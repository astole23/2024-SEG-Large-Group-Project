Frequently Asked Questions

1. General Questions

1.1 What is SHY?

SHY is a job hunting platform designed to connect job seekers with employers. Unlike other platforms, SHY focuses solely on job opportunities without any social media features.

1.2 How does SHY work?

Job seekers create a profile, upload their resumes, and apply for jobs directly through SHY. Employers can post job listings and search for candidates based on skills and experience.

1.3 Is SHY free to use?

Yes, SHY is free for job seekers. Employers can post a limited number of jobs for free, with premium options available for additional features.

2. Account & Profile

2.1 How do I create an account?

Simply click on the "Sign Up" button, enter your details, and follow the instructions to create your profile.

2.2 Can I edit my profile after creating it?

Yes, you can edit your profile at any time by navigating to the "Profile Settings" section in your dashboard.

2.3 How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account by going to "Account Settings" and selecting the "Delete Account" option. Please note that this action is irreversible.

3. Job Applications

3.1 How do I apply for jobs on SHY?

Browse job listings, select a job that interests you, and click the "Apply Now" button. You may be asked to submit a resume and answer a few screening questions.

3.2 Can I track my job applications?

Yes, all your applications are saved under the "My Applications" section, where you can check their status.

3.3 What happens after I apply for a job?

Employers will review your application and may contact you for further steps, such as an interview.

4. Employers & Job Listings

4.1 How do I post a job on SHY?

Employers can create an account, navigate to the "Post a Job" section, and enter the job details. Listings may require approval before going live.

4.2 Can I edit or remove a job posting?

Yes, job postings can be edited or removed from the "Manage Listings" section in your employer dashboard.

4.3 Are there any fees for posting jobs?

Basic job postings are free, but we offer premium options for increased visibility and additional features.

5. Security & Privacy

5.1 Is my personal information safe on SHY?

Yes, SHY follows strict data protection policies. Please review our Privacy Policy for more details.

5.2 How do I report a fraudulent job posting?

If you come across a suspicious job posting, please use the "Report Job" button or contact our support team.

5.3 Can employers see my full profile?

Employers can only see the information you choose to make public. You can adjust visibility settings in your profile.

6. Technical Support

6.1 I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

6.2 I'm having trouble using SHY. What should I do?

If you experience any technical issues, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. If the problem persists, contact our support team.

6.3 How do I contact customer support?

You can reach out to our support team by visiting the Contact Us page.
