Help Centre

Find answers to common questions and issues below.

Getting Started

How to create an account: To create an account, click on the 'Sign Up' button on the homepage, fill in your details, and submit the form.

Logging in and out: To log in, enter your credentials on the login page. To log out, click on your profile menu and select 'Logout'.

Updating your profile: Go to your profile settings, make the necessary changes, and click 'Save'.

Account Management

Resetting your password: Click on 'Forgot Password' on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions in the email you receive.

Changing your email address: Go to 'Account Settings', update your email, and confirm the change via the verification link sent to your new email.

Deleting your account: To delete your account, go to 'Account Settings' and select 'Delete My Account'. This action is irreversible.

Job Applications

How to apply for jobs: Browse job listings, click on a job, and follow the application instructions on the job page.

Tracking your applications: Go to 'My Applications' to see the status of your submitted job applications.

Editing an application: If the job allows edits, you can update your application under 'My Applications' before the deadline.

Technical Support

Troubleshooting login issues: If you can't log in, ensure your credentials are correct. If the problem persists, reset your password.

Browser compatibility: Our site works best on Chrome, Firefox, and Edge. If you experience issues, try clearing your cache.

Reporting a bug: If you encounter a bug, contact support with a description of the issue and screenshots if possible.
